About

The club has supported people who are keen to grow and develop through the services it provides

  • 2018-2019

    Our Humble Beginnings

    It was a thought of one of the students at Makerere University to have a student led club where students organise themselves and come up with ideas which they implement inorder to build themselves and others economically, socially and politically.

  • November 2019

    A Club is Born

    With the help of the Dean of students, Makerere University the club was launched under the Makerere University Guidance and counselling department.

  • January 2020

    Transition to Full Service

    Through the commitment and hardwork of the club team, the club's activities started taking place upto date with the help of the club's patron hence making the dream come true.

  • August 2021

    Phase Two Expansion

    The club's activities have been extended to other universities and have run smoothly.
